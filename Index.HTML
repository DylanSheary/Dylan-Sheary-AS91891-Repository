Intoduction

From hundred year old classical music to modern rap tracks, the piano is extremely well rooted in all genres of music and music culture as a whole.
Even those who find little value in music as an art form know that instruments as impactful as the piano have had a monumental amount of influence over humanity’s culture as a whole.
I for one am delighted to say that I can play this tremendously meaningful instrument, and that through this website, I can share some of its fascinating history with you.


However, if you are unfamiliar with the piano itself, I can also help you with this. Featuring 88 keys over seven octaves, the piano is as musically versatile as it is meaningful. Unlike more standard instruments, the piano isn’t part of any single part of the three types of instrument, (string woodwind and percussion) but rather a blend of percussion and string, resulting in a whole new sound when it was first invented in ~1700.
